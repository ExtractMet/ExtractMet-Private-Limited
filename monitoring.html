AI-Enabled Remote Process Monitoring

Real-time telemetry, anomaly detection, and predictive alerts for blast furnaces, EAFs, and submerged arc furnaces—delivering expert process guidance without an on-site consultant.

Platform

Your furnace data, always in expert hands

ExtractMet's monitoring platform ingests real-time process data from your existing instrumentation—no hardware replacement required. Our AI models, trained on fundamental metallurgical process knowledge and historical plant data, detect anomalies and prescribe corrective actions before problems escalate.

  • Connects to existing DCS/SCADA via standard OPC-UA, Modbus, or REST APIs
  • Real-time key performance indicator (KPI) dashboard accessible anywhere
  • AI anomaly detection with alert classification and root cause analysis
  • Predictive maintenance alerts for critical equipment (electrodes, tuyeres, cooling)
  • Weekly process reports with recommendations from metallurgical experts

Supported Processes

What we monitor

Raceway temperature, hot metal temperature and chemistry, burden distribution, gas utilisation efficiency (CO utilisation), and shaft pressure differential. Predictive detection of hanging, slipping, and tuyere damage.
Electrode position and immersion depth, power and energy input per heat, bath temperature estimation, oxygen and carbon injection optimisation, and off-gas composition for foaming slag control. Heat time and melt-out prediction.
Electrode slip and breakage detection, burden resistance monitoring, specific energy consumption, tap chemistry prediction, and slag level estimation. Charge mix recommendation for optimal slag basicity and alloy recovery.
Lance position and blowing pattern optimisation, dynamic charge calculation, end-point carbon and temperature prediction, and slopping detection via off-gas and vibration analysis.
