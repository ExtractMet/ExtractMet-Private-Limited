Decarbonization & Green Steelmaking

Slashing carbon emissions in iron and steel—through intelligent modelling, green hydrogen integration, and a comprehensive roadmap to net zero. Validated with real plant data.

What We Offer

Unlocking green technologies for iron & steel

The iron and steel sector contributes ~8% of global CO₂ emissions. ExtractMet designs evidence-based, plant-specific decarbonization pathways—combining fundamental thermodynamics, process simulation, and real operational data—to make the transition economically viable.

  • Green hydrogen DRI integration feasibility studies
  • EAF optimisation for scrap-based and DRI-charged operations
  • Blast furnace to DRI/EAF transition modelling
  • CO₂ capture & utilisation pathway assessment
  • Carbon credit monetisation and ESG reporting support

Why decarbonize now?

Steel is among the hardest sectors to decarbonize, yet it faces the tightest regulatory and market pressure. Early movers will benefit from lower carbon credit costs, access to green steel premiums, and ESG financing advantages.

Our team has modelled over a dozen large-scale steel plant transitions—combining thermodynamic process modelling with actual plant data to produce actionable, bankable roadmaps.

Our Methodology

From baseline to net-zero roadmap

Scope 1 and 2 emission quantification using plant mass/energy balances, raw material composition analysis, and operating data reconciliation. We identify the top three emission hotspots within your value chain.
Comparative modelling of hydrogen DRI, CCUS, electrification, and operational efficiency levers against your specific plant configuration. Each option is evaluated on technical feasibility, CO₂ abatement potential, CAPEX/OPEX, and implementation timeline.
First-principles process simulation (steady-state and dynamic) validated against real plant data. We quantify sensitivity to feedstock variability, energy prices, and hydrogen availability to de-risk investment decisions.
A time-bound, milestone-driven roadmap with capital allocation recommendations, regulatory compliance mapping, and a full financial model including NPV, IRR, and payback under multiple carbon price scenarios.
